About HRMS Platform

The Health Risk Management System (HRMS) is a comprehensive platform designed to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and insurance analytics. Our system empowers medical professionals with data-driven insights to optimize patient care and financial outcomes.

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare risk management through innovative technology that transforms complex insurance data into actionable insights, ultimately improving patient outcomes while reducing financial risks for healthcare institutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Vardhan Yadav and Apoorv Khanna visionary for HRMS, HRMS was born from the recognition that healthcare providers needed better tools to navigate the increasingly complex insurance landscape.

Expert Team

Our team combines decades of healthcare, insurance, and data science expertise to deliver unparalleled insights.

Secure Platform

HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with enterprise-grade security to protect sensitive patient data.

Innovative Solutions

Cutting-edge AI and machine learning models trained on millions of healthcare data points.

This dashboard updates in real-time with the latest insurance market data. Use the filters to view statistics specific to your specialty, location, or patient demographics. Risk Assessment Calculator

About Our Algorithm

This tool utilizes machine learning trained on millions of healthcare insurance cases to assess various medical, demographic, and financial factors. The algorithm considers diagnosis codes, treatment plans, age, location, and insurance history to generate a comprehensive risk analysis and coverage recommendations.
